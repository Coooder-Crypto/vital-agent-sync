# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include demonstrating empathy, respecting differing opinions and experiences, giving and accepting constructive feedback, taking responsibility for mistakes, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes sexualized language or imagery, trolling or insulting comments, harassment, publishing private information without permission, and conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior through a private repository security or maintainer contact channel. Do not use a public issue when a report contains personal information.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.
